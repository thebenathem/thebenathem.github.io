Where We Take Root

Coming January 2027

For Hale, every future has already been planned. Every path has already been chosen. When catastrophe tears apart the only life he has ever known, survival means trusting someone utterly unlike himself.

Thraen has spent his life preserving harmony wherever he finds it. He has never encountered another intelligent species before. He certainly never expected one frightened, stubborn human to change the course of his own life.

As two strangers struggle to understand one another across impossible differences, they slowly discover that home is not always somewhere you return to. Sometimes, it is someone who quietly chooses to stay.

A quiet queer science-fiction romance about loneliness, trust, compassion and the slow work of learning to belong.
