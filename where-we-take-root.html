Where We
Take Root

Hale has spent his life tending the fragile beginnings of other people’s futures. Thraen has spent his listening for the hidden harmony beneath every living thing. When disaster strands Hale far from everything familiar, survival depends on an alien whose language he cannot understand—and whose kindness asks for nothing in return.

As trust grows through shared shelter, small rituals and the quiet work of learning one another, two lonely people begin to discover that home may not be a place at all. It may be the person who hears the parts of you no one else noticed.
