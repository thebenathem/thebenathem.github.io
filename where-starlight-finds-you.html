Where Starlight
Finds You

The war is over.

The scars it left behind are not.

Across a galaxy struggling to rebuild, two survivors carry very different burdens. One is human. The other belongs to a people forever changed by the conflict that reshaped countless worlds. Though separated by culture, history, and everything they once called home, chance draws them together when they need someone most.

Neither expects the journey that follows.

As they travel between recovering worlds and forgotten corners of the galaxy, quiet moments begin to matter more than grand adventures. Shared meals become conversations. Silence becomes understanding. Friendship slowly deepens into something neither dares to name.

But healing is rarely a straight path.

Old griefs have a way of following those who try to outrun them, and some wounds cannot be forgotten simply because the fighting has ended. To build a future together, they must first decide whether the past defines who they are—or whether they can choose something new.

Tender, hopeful, and deeply character-driven, Where Starlight Finds You is a slow-burn queer science-fiction romance about finding family beyond blood and discovering love where you least expect it.
