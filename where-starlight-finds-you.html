Take Me With You

Coming June 2027

For generations, stories have been told of a solitary traveller who appears wherever the oldest roads of the galaxy begin to fail.

When a young human leaves behind the only life they have ever known, they unexpectedly cross paths with the last Waywarden—an ancient traveller who has spent millennia quietly preserving the forgotten pathways of a vanished civilisation.

As impossible distances unfold and an unlikely companionship begins to grow, both discover that even after thousands of years, it is never too late to find somewhere—or someone—to call home.
