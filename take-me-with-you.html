COMING SOON

Take Me
With You

For generations, stories have been told of a solitary traveller who appears wherever the oldest roads of the galaxy begin to fail. Some believe he is a myth. Others call him the Waywarden.

Far from those ancient routes, a young colonist leaves the only life he has known rather than remain in a future already decided for him. His journey should end in silence. Instead, it leads him to the oldest traveller the galaxy has ever known—and to a companionship neither of them expected.

As forgotten stations awaken and impossible distances unfold, two souls separated by age, history and everything they understand about home must decide what it means to choose one another.
