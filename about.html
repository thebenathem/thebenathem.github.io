About the author

Ben Athem

Ben Athem writes quiet, character-driven queer science-fiction romance about people learning to become home for one another.

Whether set aboard a failing colony station, an ancient alien vessel, or beneath unfamiliar stars, his stories are less interested in spectacle than in the ordinary acts of kindness that allow people to survive extraordinary worlds.

His work explores belonging, loneliness, hope, curiosity and compassion, often through unlikely relationships between people who begin by misunderstanding one another and end by choosing, day after day, to remain.

Rather than relying on destiny or dramatic declarations, his romances grow from countless small moments: a shared silence, an offered hand and the decision to notice someone else’s needs before they are spoken.

At the heart of every story is the belief that kindness is never weakness, understanding is always worth pursuing and love matters because it is freely chosen.

Home is not always somewhere you return to.

Sometimes, it is someone who quietly chooses to stay.
