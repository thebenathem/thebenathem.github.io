For the readers who get there first

Join the ARC Team

ARC readers get my books before anyone else, in exchange for an honest review when release day comes. No deadlines, no pressure to love it — just readers who care about quiet, character-driven stories and are willing to tell other readers what they found.

What you'll get

  • An early copy of each new release, usually a few weeks before publication
  • Occasional notes on where a story came from, sent to ARC readers first
  • A quiet corner of the process most readers don't see

What I'm asking

  • Read in your own time — before release if you can manage it
  • Leave an honest review wherever you usually post them
  • Tell me if a book wasn't for you. Honest feedback helps more than perfect stars
