Quiet, character-driven speculative fiction about people learning to become home for one another.

Whether set aboard a failing colony station, an ancient alien vessel, or somewhere touched by magic, these stories are less interested in extraordinary worlds than in the ordinary acts of kindness that allow people to survive them.
